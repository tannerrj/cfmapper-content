Region's longname: The Kingdom of Scorn

Region description: Nestled snugly in a sheltered bay at the west of the Imperial Highway, Scorn is a safe and welcoming place for those who pass by. Its position on the Imperial highway allows it to maintain its importance as a stopping off point between the other major cities along the route and an attractive location for trade and commerce. This combined with the sheltered harbour and extensive shipping lines, ensures that Scorn remains a key crossroads bustling with activity, long after its former military dominance has diminished.
