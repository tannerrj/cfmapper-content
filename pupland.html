Region's longname: Pupland

Region description: The island of Pup Land (also known as the Shining Kingdom) was founded by the great hero Siegfried and later ruled by Lord Eureca. It extends from Old Pupland in the west to the Shining Wall on the East, from the badlands North of Nurnberg to Violet Island, southernmost of the Rainbow Islands.
