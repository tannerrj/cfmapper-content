    • scorn/ScornHero

      The Scorn Royalty Quest consists of various items to find or monsters to slay to get rewards, including :

      • shields
      • weapons
      The quests starts in Scorn Castle.

      • Dragonlord's Lair (Ruggilli's Whisker is here)

    • wolfsburg/Lursendis

      Lursendis wishes to eat a caramel made by his friend Farnass.
      No combat required, player just needs to figure where to find the NPCs or items.
      The reward is a special item food-related.

      Starts in: Lursendis's house

      Linked maps:

      • Castle Kitchen (Farnass is here.)
      • Farnass (Farnass's old house, which exploded. No special hint.)
      • Gren's House (Entrance to an underground tavern.)
      • Lursendis's house (Quest starts and ends here.)
      • Mushroom Quest (Here is the mushroom Farnass needs.)
      • Scorn Castle, Entrance (Password for the kitchen.)
      • Scorn's Trappers (Hints on what the chickens need.)
      • Sentrio's farmhouse (Place to get the eggs, assuming the player has the right items for that.)
      • Sentrio's House (Sentrio, scientist and chicken owner. Will give location of the farm.)
      • Underground Tavern (Place to get hints on where to find Farnass.)
      • Wood House (Hospital and entrance to the trappers.)
