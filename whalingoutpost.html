Region's longname: Whaling Outpost

Region description: Also known as Terra Glaci in Old Navarian, this frozen wasteland is located on the far edge of the known world. The "Land of Frost" has been partially settled by those brave (or foolish) enough to fend off the Polar Bears, Ice elementals, Frost Dragons and similar monstrosities. Those in residence make a living (well, mere existence) out of whaling, which is why many of the nearby islands make up the Whaling Outpost. No one knows for sure who or how many reside around here, as the appetite of any wondering beast leaves countless dwellings empty and numerous ships at the bottom of the sea.
