Region's longname: The Sultanate of Darcap

Region description: Isolated from the rest of the region by an extensive mountain range to the south, and possessing a massive natural harbour, Darcap is a small, but easily defensible town, dominated by a pair of powerful guilds. The town is also noted for its circus, whose reputation draws visitors from all over the world.
