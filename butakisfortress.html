Region's longname: Fortress of Lord Butakis

Region description: The Fortress of Lord Butakis stands as a testament to the determination of Butakis to settle and secure the area called Lake Country. This bastion of security provides a hard line of defense against the demons, dragons, rogue wizard faction and agents of chaos that plague this land. While the stronghold stands in defiance of disorder, there seems to be dark forces at work from the inside; slowly whittling away the foundation of what the Fortress represents. As a result of this, security of the Fortress has greatly increased in the past years. A few have grown concerned over the very crowded prison filled with accused spies, thieves, and worse.
