Region's longname: Lone Town

Region description: Located on the westernmost peninsula of the Shining Kingdom in Pup Land, Lone Town is a somewhat isolated but very well established settlement. Multiple shops for any visitor along with a Dragon Terminal, Training Center, and Guild Halls make Lone Town not only a destination for commerce and adventure but also full-time residence.
